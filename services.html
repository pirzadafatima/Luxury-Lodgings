Experience

Stay fit with our state-of-the-art gym and golf course or let our experienced travel guides show you the local treasures. With something for everyone, our services are designed to provide the ultimate experience of relaxation, excitement, and adventure. Discover the luxury that knows no bounds at Luxury Lodgings.!



In-Dining Experience

Our resort boasts a luxurious on-site restaurant where you can indulge in the most delectable cuisine prepared by our skilled chefs. Whether you're in the mood for a romantic dinner for two or a family feast, our restaurant has something to offer for everyone. Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, our restaurant is the perfect place to savor mouthwatering dishes and impeccable service. Prices range from $25 to $60 per person depending on the menu items.

Sports Experience

Golf: Our resort features a pristine golf course that offers a challenging yet enjoyable experience for golf enthusiasts. With lush green fairways and stunning views, our golf course is the perfect place to unwind and practice your swing. The course is open from 7 am to 5 pm daily and is available to guests for a fee of $50 per person for a round of 18 holes.




Fitness Experience

Gym: Stay fit and healthy during your stay at Luxury Lodgings with our state-of-the-art gym facilities. Our gym is equipped with the latest fitness equipment and is open 24 hours a day for your convenience. Whether you want to lift weights or do cardio, our gym has everything you need to stay in shape. Price is $30 dollar per hour.

Water Park

Our resort features an exciting water park with thrilling rides and slides for all ages. The water park is open from 10 am to 6 pm, and the prices are $50 per adult and $30 per child under 12 years old. Children under 3 years old can enter for free.




Note: All services will be automatically available to you for the entirety of your stay for an additional charge of only $200.
